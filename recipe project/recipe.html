Cooking Chapati


to cook soft and tender chapati you will follow this recipe.

ingredients

  • 2kg of flour
  • water
  • cooking oil
  • sugar and eggs

recipe

  • mix the ingredient to make a dough
  • kneed the dough then roll into circle
  • roll the circled dough to circles shape
  • put it on a pan while marinating with oil
  • turn evenly on both side for crispy and brown colour
